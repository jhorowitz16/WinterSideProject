Mechamario and Waifuu

The OU suspect test for Sablenite and Shadow Tag ended with the following results: Ban 99 to Do Not Ban 86 for Sablenite (53.5%) and Ban 165 to Do Not Ban 20 for Shadow Tag (89.2%). The minimum to ban was 60%, thus Sablenite is safe and Shadow Tag is banned from OU.

Meanwhile, PU is testing Vigoroth and Exeggutor through the 30th. Vigoroth's combination of Bulk Up + Slack Off and Exeggutor's raw power and versatility have brought them into question. Players will need to reach 2800 coil with a B value of 9.0 to be eligible to vote. Good luck!

—Eevee General on Dec 18, 2015

Ultimate Gaming Month

From December 19th to January 18th, the six chat gaming rooms on PS will all be participating in a fun event called Ultimate Gaming Month! During UGM, the Mafia, Scavengers, Survivor, Battle Dome, Trivia, and Game Corner rooms will all give out points for participation in events and general games. Workshops will also be held in Mafia, Survivor, and Battle Dome on the 19th of December.

The top placers will receive prizes, including a PS custom avatar for the overall winner! If you haven't yet, check out the involved rooms and make sure to be there during the month for lots of fun!

—Dell on Dec 14, 2015
